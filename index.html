Strawberry Shortcake Recipe

Make this easy strawberry shortcake and impress your friends. Macerate fresh strawberries in sugar and spoon over tender biscuits, then top with whipped cream. It'll be your go-to strawberry shortcake recipe!

This is really tasty!

Lists of Ingredients

  • Fresh strawberries
  • Suger,baking powder,Salt, baking powder and all-purpose flour
  • Milk,unsalted butter,Heavy whipping cream and Vanilla extract

Methods

  1. Prep the strawberries
  2. Macerate the strawberries in sugar
  3. Mash some of the strawberries
  4. Combine the dry ingredients for the biscuits
  5. Work in the butter and Add the wet ingredients together
  6. Chill the dough and Bake the biscuits
  7. Make the whipped cream
